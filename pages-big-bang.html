Thus, the fundamental laws of nature, as we know them, obey, and when discovered by mankind, testify to, God's "spoken" Order in the Universe. Gen 1:1. However, what happened prior to the hot dense singularity that produced the entirety of a seemingly (observable) infinite universe? What caused the density for unknown times being stable prior to the big bang theory kicking in.
